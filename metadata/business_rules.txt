EMPLOYEE STATUS RULES

Active employee means emp_status_code = 'A'

Terminated employee means emp_status_code = 'T'

Leave employee means emp_status_code = 'L'

Death employee means emp_status_code = 'D'


PAYMENT RULES

Verified payment means payment_status = 'V'

Pending payment means payment_status = 'P'

Historical payment means payment_status = 'H'

Open Payment means (payment_end_date is Null or payment_end_date > current date)


DEFERRED BENEFIT RULES

Deferred Vested means deferred_benefit_status = 'DF'

Voided In Payment means deferred_benefit_status = 'VP'

Voided Rehire means deferred_benefit_status = 'VR'

Voided Information means deferred_benefit_status = 'VI'


JOIN RULES

emp_status joins payment using employee_id

emp_status joins deferred_benefit using employee_id

payment joins deferred_benefit using employee_id

job_history joins emp_status using employee_id
